Code for "Age-of-Information based Scheduling in Multiuser Uplinks with Stochastic Arrivals: A POMDP Approach".

CONTENTS:
1. aoi_main
2. po_dp_theo: solve analytical results of the optimal policy
3. po_dp_simu: solve simulation results of the optimal policy
4. beli_prod: generate finite sets of belief states
5. mpp_theo: solve analytical results of MPP
6. mpp_simu: solve simulation results of MPP
7. sigl_func: solve the state transition function and observation function of each end node
8. mpf_simu: solve simulation results of MPF
9. map_simu: solve simulation results of MAP
10. rdp_simu: solve simulation results of RDP
